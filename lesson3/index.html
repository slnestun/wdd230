Purpose

The purpose of the website is to introduce you to Bakery Cupcake. A bakery that produces amazing cupcakes, those kinds of cupcakes you always imagine, but you never eat them. We make our product with the finest ingredients that people can buy. We use organic flours, sugars, and eggs. Our cupcakes are fresh and made on the day because we believe that quality comes with freshness and love. Through our website, you will see from our popular Ferrero Rocher cupcake to our classics vanilla cupcakes. Each picture will sure make many mouths water. Would you like to be our next client?

Audience

Our audience will be families, friends, individuals, and event planners looking for a new flavor of cupcakes made with organic and fresh products and with love. Our customers look to stand out for their events, from breakfast meetings and afternoon coffee breaks to birthday celebrations and employee recognition events. They love sweets and are looking to experience new flavors that are modern and popular. There is no range of age because cupcakes are for everybody and any time. But nowadays, everybody searches everything on the internet, from places to go on vacation to stores to buy cupcakes to celebrate special events.
