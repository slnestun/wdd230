Target Audience

The target audience of the villa-crespo.com website is all the people who live in Argentina. It also includes the tourists from the country and abroad that came on holidays. This site will help them to know all the local businesses in the district, historical sites, and places most visited.

Who: Tourists, local businesses, residents, and everybody who wants to know more about Villa Crespo

Age: 18 to 100

Technology: Any device or smartphone that has internet access

Motivation: Tourist may come to check out, places most visited, historical sites and local businesses. Residents may want to know more their district and Local businesses may want to reach more clients.
